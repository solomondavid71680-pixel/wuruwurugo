WuruwuruGo is Nigeria's most advanced fake bank alert detector and betting odds manipulation checker. It analyzes SMS text, bank receipt images, and betting codes to expose fraud — all running entirely on your device with no internet needed for analysis.

It runs 25+ checks on the alert text: bank-specific format matching for 28 Nigerian banks, reference number pattern validation, sender ID verification, kobo decimal checks, spelling analysis, balance field detection, date/time format, account masking patterns, and known fraud app signatures (Flash Fund, ProBank).
